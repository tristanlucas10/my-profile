Student Life

My life now is something that I couldn’t have imagined a year ago. I had never been to France till last summer and now I am living right next to the capital. Paris was so much more than I thought it would be. From the stereotypes to the riots, I had low expectations for Paris, but when I arrived I couldn't believe it. I love my life at ESSEC and I am trying my best to lead a healthy and educated student life.


Football

I also had the good fortune to work as an assistant coach, which offered me the opportunity to develop my exercise planning, practice leading, and game preparation skills. I also studied the science of how to improve the physical and mental development of athletes during my internship at the American coaching company, Modern Age Warriors. I observed processes that involved players from Borussia Dortmund, the Los Angeles Lakers, St. Pauli, and Fehérvár FC. This helped me understand the significance of another aspect of coaching requirements related to lifestyle. With my love for the game, my playing and coaching background, and my ESSEC education, I am supporting my ultimate goal of becoming a professional football manager at one of the top football clubs in the world.
